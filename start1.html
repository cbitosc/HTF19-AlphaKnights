COSC

COSC is an open source focused tech community based in Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology. COSC conduct hackathons, bootcamps and workshops to teach students different technologies while promoting open source culture. Open Source encourages students to collaborate and helps in know how,It provides students insight into better software and also helps to work on them. The group of open source enthusiasts use and promote open source software. The, students, conduct sessions on different open source software and technologies, and teach other students about them and encourage them to use it.

NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Central Sector Scheme of Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. It provides opportunity to the student youth of 11th & 12th Class of schools at +2 Board level and student youth of Technical Institution, Graduate & Post Graduate at colleges and University level of India to take part in various government led community service activities & programmes.

Street Cause

Street Cause is an NGO comprising of students intent on doing their bit for the betterment of society.it has radically grown from being a local guild of adherents, with the objective of helping the underprivileged and the destitute in any and every way possible. Street cause cbit has provided service to the mankind since the last ten years. This club is all about community service and social service. It has a lot of events spread over the year that focus on helping out in less privileged areas and spreading awareness.

Transcendent

Transcendent is the college newspaper and the Celeritas is the college magazines. They are both entirely run by students only; everything from the writing to the designing and printing. The Transcendent team is a tight-knit team that brainstorms together, decides what pieces to go with, allots them to writers and edits the final version.
